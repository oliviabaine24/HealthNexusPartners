A physician's view of where healthcare, capital, and innovation actually meet.

Vipul Kella has spent nineteen years practicing emergency medicine while building a parallel career in health-tech investing and operating. He founded Health Nexus Partners to close the distance between three worlds that rarely communicate: clinical practice, capital, and company building.

His work is grounded in a simple premise: healthcare improves fastest when the people who understand medicine, the people who fund innovation, and the people who build companies are talking to each other directly.
