A physician's view of where healthcare, capital, and innovation actually meet.

Vipul Kella has spent nineteen years practicing emergency medicine while building a parallel career in health-tech investing and operating. He founded Health Nexus Partners to close the distance between three worlds that rarely communicate: clinical practice, capital, and company building.

ER Physician, 19 Years Venture Investor Healthcare Operator

His work is grounded in a simple premise: healthcare improves fastest when the people who understand medicine, the people who fund innovation, and the people who build companies are talking to each other directly.

Women's Health Nexus 2026

Where capital, innovation, clinical leadership, and policy converge.

Women's Health Nexus brings together investors, founders, physicians, health system leaders, policymakers, researchers, and industry executives committed to advancing the future of women's health.

This is not a traditional conference. It is a curated gathering of the leaders building, funding, adopting, and scaling the next generation of women's health innovation.

Join a community dedicated to accelerating investment, commercialization, clinical adoption, and meaningful impact across women's health.

What to Expect

High-impact content

Hear from leading investors, healthcare executives, physicians, policymakers, and founders shaping the future of women's health.

Meaningful connections

Engage with decision-makers, capital providers, health systems, innovators, and strategic partners through curated networking experiences.

Action-oriented outcomes

  • Facilitating investment into promising women's health companies
  • Creating new partnerships and pilot opportunities
  • Connecting founders with investors and health systems
  • Advancing dialogue around innovation, access, and policy
